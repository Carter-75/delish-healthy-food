CUSTOM HIGH-PROTEIN MINI PIZZA (5-INCH)

YIELD
• 1 mini pizza (4 slices)

====================
INGREDIENTS
====================

DOUGH
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 0.5 tbsp extra flour (for kneading)
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 2 tbsp unflavored protein powder
- 3/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning

TOPPINGS & FINISH
- 1 tsp olive oil (brush crust)
- 1 cup pizza sauce
- 3 tbsp parmesan cheese
- 2 imitation crab sticks, sliced
- 1 beef stick, sliced
- 2 tbsp ranch

====================
INSTRUCTIONS
====================

1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. In a bowl combine flour, baking powder, salt, protein powder, and Italian seasoning.
3. Add Greek yogurt and mix until dough forms.
4. Knead 1–2 minutes until smooth, using extra flour if sticky.

6. Press dough into a 5-inch round pizza.
7. Brush crust edge with olive oil.
8. Spread sauce over base.
9. Add parmesan, crab, and beef stick slices.
10. Drizzle ranch over top.
11. Bake 15 minutes.
12. Place directly on oven rack for 2–4 minutes to crisp.
13. Rest 2 minutes and slice into 4 pieces.

PEANUT BUTTER BASE
- 360 g fat-free vanilla Greek yogurt (plain works too)

- ¼ tsp salt (plus flaky salt for topping)

CHOCOLATE TOPPING

- 1 tsp coconut oil (optional)

BROOKIES (BROWNIE + COOKIE)

YIELD
• 12 large bars
• 1 baking pan (20x20 cm or 8x8 in)

====================
INGREDIENTS
====================

BROWNIE LAYER
- 225 g unsalted butter (melted)
- 250 g brown sugar
- 150 g powdered sugar
- 3 large eggs (~150 g)
- 90 g dark chocolate (melted)
- 50 g cocoa powder
- 50 g all-purpose flour
- 15 g vegetable oil
- 15 ml vanilla extract
- Pinch salt (~1 g)
- Chocolate chunks to taste (~100 g)

COOKIE DOUGH LAYER
- 115 g butter (melted)
- 150 g brown sugar
- 1 large egg (~50 g)
- 5 ml vanilla extract
- 190 g all-purpose flour
- 2 g baking soda
- 3 g salt
- 135 g chocolate chips

====================
INSTRUCTIONS
====================

BROWNIE BATTER
1. Mix melted butter, cocoa powder, and melted dark chocolate.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk brown sugar and eggs for about 5 minutes until glossy.
3. Add powdered sugar and mix gently.
4. Add vanilla, salt, oil, and flour. Mix just until combined.
5. Fold in chocolate chunks.

COOKIE DOUGH
6. Mix melted butter and brown sugar until creamy.
7. Add egg and vanilla and mix well.
8. Add flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix until just combined.
9. Fold in chocolate chips.

10. Preheat oven to 330–340°F (165–170°C).
11. Spread brownie batter evenly in lined baking pan.
12. Spoon cookie dough on top and lightly swirl or layer.

OREO CHEESECAKE BARS

YIELD
• 10 bars
• Silicone chocolate bar mold or small tray

====================
INGREDIENTS
====================

CREAM FILLING
- 20 Oreo cookies (remove white cream and reserve)
- 180 g cream cheese (Philadelphia style)
- 100 ml heavy cream
- 2 tbsp powdered sugar

COOKIE BASE
- Crushed Oreo cookies (the same 20 Oreo shells, without cream)
- 35 g butter (melted)

CHOCOLATE COATING
- 300 g chocolate total
  - 170 g for bottom layer
  - 130 g for top layer

====================
INSTRUCTIONS
====================

CREAM FILLING
1. Remove the white cream from 20 Oreo cookies and place into a bowl.
2. Add cream cheese, heavy cream, and powdered sugar.
3. Mix until smooth and creamy.
4. Refrigerate filling while preparing other layers.

COOKIE BASE
5. Crush Oreo cookies (without cream) into fine crumbs.
6. Add melted butter.
7. Mix until texture resembles wet sand.

BOTTOM CHOCOLATE LAYER
8. Melt 170 g chocolate.
9. Pour a thin layer into silicone molds or tray.
10. Refrigerate for 15–30 minutes until firm.

ASSEMBLY
11. Spread cream filling over hardened chocolate layer.
12. Add Oreo cookie base on top of cream layer.
13. Press lightly to compact.
14. Refrigerate for another 15–30 minutes.

TOP CHOCOLATE LAYER
15. Melt remaining 130 g chocolate.
16. Pour over bars to fully cover.
17. Refrigerate 15–30 minutes until fully set.

SERVING
18. Chill bars at least 2 hours before serving.
19. Remove from molds and slice if needed.
20. Store refrigerated.

TOPPING

Hawaiian Roll Garlic Parmesan Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients
- 12 Hawaiian sweet rolls (1 standard package)
- 4 tbsp butter (melted)
- 2 tsp garlic powder
- 2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
- 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1/2 cup pizza sauce
- 3 oz pepperoni slices
- Cooking spray (for pan)

Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Lightly spray a baking tray or baking dish.
2. Slice the Hawaiian rolls in half horizontally, keeping them connected as one slab.
3. Place the bottom half of the rolls onto the baking tray.
4. In a small bowl, mix melted butter, garlic powder, and grated parmesan.
5. Brush the garlic butter mixture evenly over the cut side of the rolls.
6. Bake for 5–7 minutes until lightly toasted.
7. Remove from oven and sprinkle 1 cup of mozzarella cheese evenly over the rolls.
8. Spread pizza sauce evenly on top of the cheese.
9. Add the remaining 1/2 cup mozzarella on top of the sauce.
10. Brush the exposed edges with more garlic butter mixture.
11. Evenly distribute pepperoni slices on top.
12. Return to oven and bake for 10–12 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.
13. Remove from oven, slice, and serve hot.
